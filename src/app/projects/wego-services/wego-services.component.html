Project Overview

With the increasing prevalence of autonomous vehicles in everyday life, we seek to utilise them as more than just rideshares or food and grocery deliveries. Need your suit pressed for a party this evening? Weâ€™ve got you covered. Need medication delivered on short notice? We can do that too.

Our goal of this project was to produce a proof of concept for a Transportation as a Service web application that would provide every day services, such as RX delivery, dry-cleaning, etc., as a part of an ever growing suite of services that under the WeGo brand.

Our autonomous vehicle vendors will be the main providers of couriers for each of the services that we intend to provide. With our software, our vendors would be able to maintain, monitor and simulate the vehicles they have provided us.

Design and Process

From pitch to POC, our team developed several service concepts to execute our TaaS with. In coming up with our service concepts we developed UCD artifacts and Web Platform canvases that would serve the foundations of determining our service. Ultimately we decided on a Dry Cleaning service.

For the first several weeks of development, we spent a lot of our time diagramming ad building the foundations with things such as marketecture, system and static and dynamic object orientated diagrams. These would be continuously updated as we learnt more about the project and would be our primary sources to reference when we considered added in new features and systems.
